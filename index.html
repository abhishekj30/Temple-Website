About Shree Datta Devasthan

H.H. Sadguru Sree Ramakrishna Kshirsagar Swami

In today's fast-paced life and modern scientific age, humanity has lost peace of mind. To achieve eternal happiness, there is a need to walk on the spiritual path. To revive Vedic religion in today's times, to remove the stigma that has come to Vedic dharma, and for the salvation of devotees, H.H. Sadguru Shri Ramakrishna Kshirsagar Maharaj incarnated.

Born on Falgun Shuddha Tritiya, Shake 1855 (February 16, 1934) in Rayatale village, Parner Taluka, Nagar District. At the tender age of 7, he had a divine vision, gained knowledge of the past, present and future, and at that very moment, he had an idea of his life's work.

Shri Datta Devasthan Trust

H.H. Sadguru Shri Ramkrishna Kshirsagar Maharaj at the behest of Shrimannrusimha Saraswati Swami's decree, established Shri Datta Devasthan Trust for the propagation of Vedas in 1974. The main aim of establishing this trust is to carry out the work of protection and preservation of Vedas.

To foster this work further, H.H. Sadguru established Vedant Vidyapeeth (University), where the Four Vedas are taught through Gurukul Pedagogy. The scope of Shri Datta Devasthan Trust encompasses Vedant Vidyapeeth, Shri Dattakshetra, Tapovan Maruti Mandir, Goshala, and many Satsanga Mandalas.
